simple and easy car stats

On the Road is your go to app for tracking a ton of different car/driving statistics. Use this app to see your average miles per gallon, cost per gallon, miles driven before filling up, as well as your total miles driven, and even a history log so you can view all of your prior fills to see what day you specifically had the best gas mileage or the cheapest gas.

get started today

1. Download On the Road onto your iOS device

2. Before your first log, make sure that your car has a full tank of gas to get accurate statistics

3. When you have a full tank, start your car's trip odometer at 0 so you can track how many miles you have driven when you fill your car up next

4. The next time you fill up your car with gas, take out your phone to enter in the miles you've driven, the amount of gallons you filled, and your total cost of gas
