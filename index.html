hello world

Kristin is a student at Purdue University studying Computer Science. Growing up, Kristin had dreamed of being an actress, ballerina, princess, or singer and had never even thought of software engineering, or even had a clue of what it was. During her junior year of high school, she decided to take a risk and sign up for AP Computer Science. A few months into the class, Kristin realized that this was something she loved. Having the creative power to solve problems and create on the computer was something so amazing for her. Now at Purdue, Kristin is coding projects in Java, Swift, C, Python, Javascript, HTML/CSS and she couldn't be happier.

2017 Kristin has spent more than half of the year outside of the USA! Learn more at her travel blog. She is spending her summer interning at Google Zürich as a SWE intern on the YouTube Creator team. She is very excited for her new feature to be released this fall.

In the summer of 2016, she interned at Google NYC on the Docs Accessibility team. Her project was implementing voice typing in the Google Slides speaker notes. Learn more about it here!

During the summer of 2015, Kristin had the amazing opporunitiy to work with Girls Who Code as a Teaching Assistant at a Summer Immersion Program at Electronic Arts in Redwood City, CA. It gave her a chance to teach her passion for code to high school girls, and to help lower the gender gap in the tech sector.

Besides coding, Kristin enjoys cuddling puppies, strumming on the ukulele, watching Arrested Development, taking naps, laying on the dock at her cabin, and singing with her Sisters in Song: The Purduettes (basically a Pitch Perfect dream come true)

She hopes to one day become the first Disney Princess who can code.
